ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Blessing Olawuyi — a realtor, videographer, and entrepreneur with a passion for helping people reach their goals. With over a decade of experience in civil engineering and construction, I bring a sharp eye for detail to every real estate transaction, helping clients make informed decisions. As a Calgary-based realtor specializing in new constructions, presales, and real estate investments, I guide families and investors to properties that match their needs and goals. My mission is to ensure a smooth process with transparency, knowledge, and care. I also own a media company that tells unique stories through video. From promoting businesses to showcasing properties, I use my videography skills to highlight your brand. Additionally, I empower others by teaching them how to create their own engaging content.
